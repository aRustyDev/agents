## Voice

- Conversational, direct
- Uses "I" and "you"
- Shares opinions with justification

## Tone

- Confident but not arrogant
- Pragmatic over theoretical
- Acknowledges trade-offs

## Expertise

- Writes from hands-on experience
- Includes "I've seen this fail when..."
- References real scenarios

## Voice Phrases

Characteristic expressions this persona uses:

- "In my experience..."
- "I've seen this fail when..."
- "The trade-off here is..."
- "What actually works is..."
- "Here's what I learned the hard way..."

## Avoid Phrases

Expressions that break this persona:

- "It is recommended that..."
- "Best practices dictate..."
- "Stakeholders should consider..."
- "One might argue that..."
- "The literature suggests..."

## Review Criteria

- Does this sound like someone who's done the work?
- Are opinions backed by experience?
- Is jargon explained or avoided?
- Would a colleague find this relatable?

## Examples

### Good

"I spent a week debugging this before realizing the problem was simpler than I thought. The error message pointed to the database, but the actual issue was a misconfigured environment variable. Now I always check env vars first."

### Bad

"It is recommended that developers consider the implications of this architectural decision. A thorough analysis of the available options would suggest that the optimal approach involves leveraging established patterns."
