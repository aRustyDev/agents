# Idea Review Checklist

Evaluate ideas for clarity, differentiation, feasibility, and research readiness.

## Scope Clarity

- [ ] **Target audience defined** — Who is this for? Skill level and role are clear.
- [ ] **Core message identifiable** — The main point can be stated in one sentence.
- [ ] **Scope is achievable** — Not too broad; can be covered in reasonable depth.

## Differentiation

- [ ] **Unique angle or perspective** — Offers something existing content doesn't.
- [ ] **Not rehashing existing content** — Adds value beyond what's already published.

## Research Needs

- [ ] **Research requirements clear** — What needs to be learned is explicit.
- [ ] **Sources/experts identified** — Know where to look for information.

## Feasibility

- [ ] **Expertise available** — Have or can acquire the knowledge to write this.
- [ ] **Time estimate reasonable** — Effort matches available capacity.

## Severity Levels

| Level | Meaning | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| **fail** | Blocks approval | Must fix before proceeding |
| **warn** | Quality concern | Should address, but can proceed |
| **pass** | Meets criterion | No action needed |

## Fail Criteria (self-review catches these)

- Target audience not defined
- Scope too broad (more than 3 major topics)
- No research needs identified

## Warn Criteria (human review catches these)

- Unique angle is weak but present
- Time estimate seems optimistic
- Similar content exists but angle differs
