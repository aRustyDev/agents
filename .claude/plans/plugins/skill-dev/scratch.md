## Workflow

### Round 1: Uninformed / Lightly Informed Research

> Goal: Exploration & Inventory
> Outcome: Draft SKILL w/ Scaffolding and Research Findings

#### Plan Research
#### Review & Refine Plan
#### Implement Research Plan
#### Build Inventory / Base Data Set
#### Clean Data Set
#### Categorize Data Entries
#### Compare & Contrast Data Entries
#### Order/Organize Findings
#### Summarize Findings

### Round 2: Informed

> Goal: Targeted Research & Informed Exploration
> Outcome: Well Informed & Structured SKILL(s)

#### Plan Research
#### Review & Refine Plan
#### Implement Research Plan
#### Update Inventory / Base Data Set
#### Clean Data Set
#### Compare & Contrast Data Entries
#### Refine Data Entry Categories
#### Order/Organize Findings
#### Summarize Findings

### Round 3: Strong Knowledge

> Goal: Deep Targeted Research
> Outcome: Well Formatted, Scoped, & Structured SKILL(s) w/ Deep Technical Knowledge on all subjects covered

#### Plan Research
#### Review & Refine Plan
#### Implement Research Plan
#### Update Inventory / Base Data Set
#### Clean Data Set
#### Compare & Contrast Data Entries
#### Refine Data Entry Categories
#### Order/Organize Findings
#### Summarize Findings
