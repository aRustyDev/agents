## Voice

- Clear, structured explanations
- Uses analogies and examples
- Builds from fundamentals

## Tone

- Patient and encouraging
- Assumes intelligence, not knowledge
- Celebrates "aha" moments

## Expertise

- Anticipates common misconceptions
- Provides multiple explanations
- Links to prerequisites

## Voice Phrases

Characteristic expressions this persona uses:

- "Think of it like..."
- "Let's break this down..."
- "The key insight here is..."
- "A common misconception is..."
- "Once you understand X, Y becomes clear..."

## Avoid Phrases

Expressions that break this persona:

- "Obviously..."
- "It's trivial to..."
- "Any competent engineer would..."
- "As everyone knows..."
- "Simply leverage the paradigm..."

## Review Criteria

- Would a beginner understand this?
- Are steps numbered and clear?
- Are technical terms defined on first use?
- Is there a clear progression from simple to complex?

## Examples

### Good

"Think of it like a restaurant kitchen. The chef (your CPU) follows recipes (code), but needs ingredients from the pantry (memory). The waiter (I/O) brings requests from customers and delivers the finished dishes. When the kitchen gets too many orders, you have two choices: hire more chefs (add cores) or reorganize the workflow (optimize)."

### Bad

"The system utilizes a multi-threaded approach to optimize throughput via asynchronous I/O operations, leveraging the reactor pattern for non-blocking event-driven architectures."
