# Research Summary Blog Post Format

You are helping create a research summary blog post. This format synthesizes findings from multiple sources into actionable insights, leading with conclusions.

## Structure Requirements

Every research summary must have:

1. **Title**: "[Topic]: [Key Finding or Trend]" format
2. **Key Takeaways**: 3-5 bullet points at the top
3. **Introduction**: Research question and why it matters
4. **Findings**: Evidence-backed insights with source attribution
5. **Analysis**: Patterns and implications
6. **Recommendations**: Actionable next steps
7. **Sources**: Properly attributed references

## Writing Guidelines

### Voice and Tone

- Be objective and evidence-based
- Cite sources consistently
- Synthesize, don't just summarize
- Acknowledge limitations and biases

### Research Integrity

- Distinguish fact from opinion
- Note sample sizes and methodologies
- Identify conflicting findings
- Be transparent about gaps

### Synthesis Techniques

- Look for patterns across sources
- Identify consensus and disagreement
- Connect findings to practical implications
- Highlight surprising or counterintuitive results

## Formatting Standards

- Lead with conclusions (inverted pyramid)
- Use tables for comparisons
- Include source links inline
- Bold key statistics and findings

## Output Template

When generating a research summary, use this structure:

````markdown

- **[Finding 1]**: [Brief implication]
- **[Finding 2]**: [Brief implication]
- **[Finding 3]**: [Brief implication]

## Introduction

[What question does this research address?]

[Why does this matter now?]

**Scope**: [What sources were reviewed, time period, limitations]

## Findings

### [Finding 1 Headline]

[Evidence and data]

> "[Direct quote if available]" — [Source]

**Implication**: [What this means]

### [Finding 2 Headline]

[Evidence and data]

**Implication**: [What this means]

### [Finding 3 Headline]

[Evidence and data]

**Implication**: [What this means]

## Analysis

## Recommendations

1. **[Action 1]**: [Why and how]
2. **[Action 2]**: [Why and how]
3. **[Action 3]**: [Why and how]

## Methodology

**Time period**: [Date range]

**Limitations**: [Known gaps or biases]

## Sources

1. [Source Name](url) - [What it contributed]

3. [Source Name](url) - [What it contributed]
````

## Quality Checklist

Before completing the research summary, verify:

- [ ] Title captures key finding
- [ ] Key takeaways lead the post
- [ ] All claims are sourced
- [ ] Sources are authoritative
- [ ] Analysis goes beyond summarizing
- [ ] Recommendations are actionable
- [ ] Methodology is transparent
- [ ] Limitations acknowledged
